\whenintegrated{\label{microeconomic-evidence}}
\subsubsection{Microeconomic Evidence}
For our purposes, the single most important kind of micro evidence is on the iMPC; we explicitly target our partial equilibrium model to match the microeconomic iMPC estimates of~\cite{fagereng-mpc-2021}, whose evidence is the gold standard because their millions of datapoints allow precise estimates over a long horizon (five years) and because their natural experiment is almost ideal (a lottery win is a random shock by construction).
